Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make an appointment

If you would like to make an appointment with any of our practitioners please contact our reception staff. Alternatively you can book an appointment online. Every effort will be made to accomodate your preferred time and choice of practitioner.

How do i get a copy of my record

If you would like to get a copy of your record,schedule an appointment with any of our practitionrs please contact our reception staff. Every effort will be made to ensure that you get your record as soon as possible.

Is there a charge for extra copies

There's no charge for extra copies on the first collection. Subsequent extra copies that would be collected will attract a $50 processing fee.

How do I know the right practitioner

At Mediclab, our culture is to promote efficient and effective delivery of medical services and we have the right medical personnel to take care of that.
